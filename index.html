My Journey

Designing the Cars

Using Tinkercad, I created a simplified and safe F1 car model suitable for children ages 4–12.

3D Printing

Faced with printer time limits, I printed each car over 3-hour intervals using Brampton Library's Ultimaker 2+.

Sustainability

I reduced plastic waste by optimizing support structures and internal fills while maintaining durability.

Donation

After packaging, I donated the cars to King Academy Child Care Centre, bringing joy to young car lovers.

About the Project

This initiative reflects my passion for cars, sustainability, and social good. Through 3D design, environmental awareness, and community outreach, I've created a project that merges engineering with empathy—hoping to inspire future creators, just like me.
