My Journey

Designing: I started by modeling F1 toy cars in Tinkercad. I chose simplified shapes to make them safer and child-friendly.

Printing: I used the Brampton Library’s Ultimaker 2+ printer, working around time limits and support structure challenges.

Packaging & Donation: Each car was filed for smoothness, packaged, and delivered to King Academy daycare after email outreach.

Sustainability: I minimized waste by adjusting support structures and internal fill. Cars were made light and durable with eco in mind.

About the Project

This passion project blends my love for cars, design, and giving back. I learned CAD modeling, sustainability practices, and social outreach. My goal is to keep building tools and toys that spark curiosity in kids—and show how engineering can be used for good.
